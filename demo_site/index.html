Welcome to UC Verifiable Credentials

This project is a proof-of-concept demonstrating how University of California academic records, like transcripts and degrees, can be secured and verified using blockchain technology.

Project Goals

Our mission is to combat document fraud, reduce administrative overhead, and empower students by giving them permanent, verifiable, and tamper-proof ownership of their academic achievements.
